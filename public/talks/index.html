Talks I gave at conferences/meetups

Many years ago I started giving talks for a simple reason: My employer at that time wouldn’t allow me to go to conferences. BUT they would cover all expenses if I gave a talk there. So I started giving talks, realized how much I like doing it.

Since then I have been onb the road talking about many topics I enjoy and also giving several workshops.
